Introducing FarmGard's Multi-Robot Deer Deterrence System

We are a team from Gini's Next Generation Robotics Lab at the University of Minnesota, collaborating with a local farmer to reduce deer intrusion in agricultural fields. Our approach integrates hardware, computer vision, and path planning to effectively patrol and protect farmlands.

Hardware Setup

We utilize drones outfitted with robust sensors for both day and night operation. This ensures minimal disruption to crops while maximizing coverage and detection of deer activity.

Computer Vision

Our computer vision pipeline leverages advanced deep learning models to detect, track, and classify deer in real-time, facilitating immediate responses to potential threats.

Path Planning

We developed a dynamic path planning algorithm to efficiently patrol large farmland. This balances coverage, battery life, and safe flight constraints, ensuring continuous monitoring.

Collaboration & Impact

Working closely with a local farmer, our team customizes FarmGard for real-world conditions. We aim to reduce crop damage and enhance agricultural sustainability through innovation.

Farm Robotics Challenge

FarmGard is our entry in the Farm Robotics Challenge (AIr category). We strive to showcase how multi-robot systems can effectively protect and enhance farming operations.
